PARK ZONES
Five worlds, five flavors of fear
🌑🕯️🩸
Bloodmoon Village
Cults · Saints · Occult Rituals
A cobblestone village perpetually bathed in crimson moonlight. Follow chalk sigils to the bonfire circle. The cult welcomes you — but leaving is another matter. Features: The Seance Mansion, Ritual Grounds Maze, The Saint's Trial escape room.
🔪🪓🚗
Dead End District
Slashers · Urban Horror · 80s Nightmare
Neon signs flicker. Dumpsters tip. Masked figures emerge from the fog. Classic slasher horror brought to life in a decaying urban nightmare. Features: The Prom Night Massacre, Butcher's Row Walkthrough, Chainsaw Chase Experience.
🪆🧸👁️
Uncanny Valley
Psychological · Creepy Dolls · Surreal
A suburban street that feels almost normal. Almost. The dolls blink. The children smile too wide. Mirrors show rooms that don't exist. Features: The Dollmaker's Workshop, Funhouse of Faces, The Mirror Maze.
🛸👾🌌
Space Station X
Extraterrestrial · Sci-Fi Horror
The station orbited peacefully until 1994. Now derelict, its corridors echo with alien sounds. Acid-lit and zero-gravity rooms test your limits. Features: Xenomorph Bay, The Contamination Chamber, Deep Space Freefall.
🌲🪓🦉
Camp Blackwood
Wilderness · Isolation · Creature Horror
The trees close in. The trail markers change. Something large moves in the dark between the pines. No phone signal. No escape. Features: The Cabin Massacre, Creature Hunt Trail, Blackwood Lake Terror Boat.
